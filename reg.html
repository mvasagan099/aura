Events Rule and Registration

Join our exciting events and showcase your talents!

Technical Events

Paper Presentation

  • Submit your research paper before the deadline.
  • Paper should be original and not published/presented elsewhere.
  • Plagiarism should be below 10-15%.
  • Presentation time: 3-5 minutes.

Technical Quiz

  • Register before the event starts.
  • Quiz will have multiple rounds with elimination.
  • Use of mobile phones or digital devices is prohibited.
  • Registration can be done in onspot also.

Animated Short Film

  • A short film or animation on a theme of your choice.
  • The content should be published or not prevented
  • Duration: 5-10 minutes.
  • Teams of 2 members can participate.
  • Film should be submitted before the deadline.
  • Shortlisted film/animation will be informed before event date.
  • Only shortlisted content will be allowed on stage.

Non-Technical Events

Fun Games

  • Teams of 2-4 members can participate.
  • Games will be announced on the spot.
  • Winners will be decided based on points scored.

Debate Expert

  • Teams of 1 members can participate.
  • Topic will be announced on the spot.
  • Debate will be timed (2-3 minutes per team).
  • Judges' decision will be final.

Ad Zap

  • Teams of 2-3 members can participate.
  • Create an advertisement for a given product/service.
  • Presentation time: 3-5 minutes.
  • Judges will evaluate creativity and marketing skills.

Word Hunt

  • Individuals can participate.
  • Find and write down as many words as possible within a given time limit.
  • Words should be at least 4 letters long.
  • Winner will be decided based on the number of correct words found.

E-Sports

  • No external assistance or cheat software allowed.
  • Register on the official platform before the tournament.
  • Devices and software may be checked before/after matches.
